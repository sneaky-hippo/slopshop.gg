How It Works

Five steps to a smarter organization.

01
Create a DreamHive and invite your team
Takes 4 minutes. Set up your shared namespace, choose a vertical template, and send invite links. No IT required.
02
Everyone writes memories
Meetings, decisions, research, code — any format. The Memory Hub captures it all and associates it to your shared namespace automatically.
03
Every night, the Dream Engine runs collective BiOtA synthesis
While the team sleeps, DreamHive's 9-stage REM cycle processes every shared memory — surfacing patterns, resolving contradictions, and generating compound insights.
04
Wake up to a REM Brief
New insights, connections, and predictions your team never saw. Delivered to everyone's inbox by 8 AM. The kind of strategic clarity that used to require a consultant.
05
The knowledge compounds
Every night the system gets smarter. After 30 days it knows your org. After 90 days it anticipates your org. After 180 days, it is your org's institutional memory.
